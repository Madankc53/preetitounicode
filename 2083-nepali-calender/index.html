Important for 2083 BS: This year has a rare Mala Maas (Adhik Maas) — an intercalary lunar month — between Jestha 3 and Ashadh 1 (May 17–June 15, 2026). Because of this extra month, Dashain and Tihar fall significantly later on the English calendar. Saturday (शनिबार) is Nepal's official weekly holiday. ❓ Frequently Asked Questions — 2083 BS

Dashain 2083 Ghatasthapana (Day 1) falls on Sunday, October 11, 2026 (Ashoj 25, 2083 BS). Vijaya Dashami (Tika) falls on Tuesday, October 20, 2026 (Kartik 3, 2083 BS). Due to Mala Maas in 2083, Dashain falls much later than usual on the English calendar.
Tihar 2083 Bhai Tika — the main final day — falls on Wednesday, November 11, 2026 (Kartik 25, 2083 BS). This is later than previous years because of the Mala Maas intercalary month in 2083 BS.
Yes. Saturday (शनिबार) is Nepal's official weekly holiday. Government offices, banks, schools, and most businesses are closed on Saturdays. Sunday is a regular working day in Nepal — unlike most Western countries.
Mala Maas (Adhik Maas / Purushottam Maas) is an intercalary month inserted into the BS lunisolar calendar. In 2083 BS it falls between Jestha 3 (May 17, 2026) and Ashadh 1 (June 15, 2026). This pushes Dashain and Tihar about one month later on the English calendar. No auspicious marriage dates fall during Mala Maas.
Nepali year 2083 BS starts on Baisakh 1, 2083 — Tuesday, April 14, 2026 and ends on Chaitra 30, 2083 — April 13, 2027 AD.
Republic Day (Ganatantra Diwas) 2083 falls on Friday, May 29, 2026 (Jestha 15, 2083 BS). It is a national public holiday celebrating Nepal becoming a federal democratic republic in 2008.
Key auspicious marriage dates in 2083 BS: Baisakh 7–8 (Apr 20–21), Baisakh 22–25 (May 5–8), Jestha 1–2 (May 15–16). No auspicious dates during Mala Maas (Jestha 3 – Ashadh 1). Always verify with an official NPNS Panchanga.
Constitution Day (Sambidhan Diwas) 2083 falls on Saturday, September 19, 2026 (Ashoj 3, 2083 BS). It also falls on a Saturday — Nepal's weekly holiday — making it a double holiday.
Chhath Parva 2083 falls in Kartik 2083, in late October or early November 2026. Verify the exact date against the official MoHA gazette.
Baisakh 2083 BS has 31 days, running from April 14, 2026 to May 14, 2026. The number of days in each Nepali month varies every year as set by the Nepal Panchanga Nirnayak Samiti.
