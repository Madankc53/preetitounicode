❓ FAQ

Why does Preeti text show as gibberish?
Preeti is an ASCII-based legacy font. Without the Preeti font installed, each character shows its ASCII fallback. Unicode works on all devices, websites, and apps — no font needed.
Are my documents stored anywhere?
No. All processing is done locally in your browser. Your text never leaves your device. Safe for legal, confidential, or government documents.
What's different about i, q, and special keys in Preeti?
In Preeti: i = ष् (with virama), if = ष, q = त्र, em = झ, cf = आ, cf] = ओ. These multi-character sequences are handled automatically by the pre-rules engine.
How do I type ि (i-matra) correctly?
In Preeti, press l before the consonant: e.g., ls gives कि. The converter automatically repositions ि to follow the consonant in Unicode.
What about Kantipur and PCS Nepali fonts?
Both are supported via their own mapping tables. Select from the font dropdown — Kantipur uses k=क, l=ख while Preeti uses s=क, v=ख.
