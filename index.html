❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is Preeti font and why convert it?
Preeti is a legacy ASCII-based Nepali font used before Unicode became standard. Text in Preeti appears as gibberish without that font. Unicode works everywhere — on all devices, websites, and apps.
Is my document stored anywhere?
No. Everything runs locally in your browser. Your text and files are never uploaded to any server.
What are Kantipur and PCS Nepali?
Like Preeti, these are older Nepali fonts used in government and publishing. This tool supports all three.
Can I use this offline?
Yes! Install as a PWA and use without internet — perfect for remote areas.
How do I convert Nepali dates?
Use our free Date Converter to switch between BS and AD instantly.
How do I share my conversion?
The URL updates automatically — just copy and share the address bar link.
