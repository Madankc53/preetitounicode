Frequently Asked Questions

What is Preeti font and why convert it?
Preeti is a legacy ASCII-based Nepali font used before Unicode became standard. Text written in Preeti appears as gibberish on any system without that font installed. Unicode Devanagari works on all devices, websites, and apps without any special fonts.
Is my document stored anywhere?
No. Everything runs in your browser using JavaScript. Your text and files are never sent to any server. This makes the tool safe for legal, government, and confidential documents.
Does .docx conversion preserve formatting?
Paragraph structure, bold, italic, and headings are preserved. Complex layouts like multi-column sections or embedded charts may not fully carry over — for those cases, convert the text and manually reformat in Word.
Is there a file size limit?
No server-side limit since everything runs locally. Very large files (50MB+) may be slow depending on your device's processing power.
Can I use this offline?
Yes. Once the page has loaded, it works without an internet connection. The conversion logic is pure JavaScript with no external API calls.
