❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is Preeti font and why convert it?
Preeti is a legacy ASCII-based Nepali font used before Unicode became standard. Text in Preeti appears as gibberish without that font. Unicode works everywhere — on all devices, websites, and apps.
Is my document stored anywhere?
No. Everything runs locally in your browser. Your text and files are never uploaded to any server, making this tool safe for legal and confidential documents.
What are Kantipur and PCS Nepali?
Like Preeti, these are older Nepali fonts used in government and publishing. This tool supports all three, making it the most complete converter available.
Can I use this offline?
Yes! This is a PWA (Progressive Web App). Install it on your phone or PC and use it without internet — perfect for field work in remote areas.
How do I share my conversion?
The URL updates automatically with your text and settings. Just copy the URL from your address bar and share it — the recipient will see exactly what you see.
