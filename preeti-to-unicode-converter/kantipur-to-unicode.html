What is Preeti font? Why convert to Unicode?

Preeti is a legacy Nepali font widely used in the 90s and 2000s for digital documents, but it lacks standard encoding. Unicode (Devanagari) ensures text displays on any device, searchable, and indexable by Google. Converting Preeti to Unicode makes your content future-proof, accessible for web, and improves SEO rankings. Over 10 million Nepali speakers benefit from standard Unicode. Our bidirectional converter also supports Unicode→Preeti for backward compatibility with legacy systems.
