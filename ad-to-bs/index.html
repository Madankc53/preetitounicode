How to Convert AD to BS

  1. Type the AD year (e.g. 2026).
  2. Type the month number (1 = January, 12 = December).
  3. Type the day. The BS date appears instantly with Nepali month name.
  4. Use 📋 Copy or 🔗 Share to save or send the result.
