📌 Frequently Asked Questions – AD to BS Conversion

❓ How do I convert an English date (AD) to Nepali (BS)?
Simply enter your AD date in YYYY-MM-DD format (e.g., 2026-05-16) and click “Convert AD → BS”. The tool instantly shows the exact Nepali calendar date.
❓ What is the difference between AD and BS?
Gregorian (AD) is the international calendar. Bikram Sambat (BS) is the official Nepali calendar, approximately 56 years and 8 months ahead of AD.
❓ Why do Nepali months have variable lengths (28–32 days)?
Bikram Sambat is lunisolar. Month lengths adjust to moon phases and solar transit. Our built‑in table captures these variations.
❓ Can I trust this AD to BS converter for official use?
Absolutely. The month‑length data comes from the standard Nepali panchanga used by government offices. Thousands rely on it for passports, visa applications, and legal documents.
