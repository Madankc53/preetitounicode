📚 About INR to NPR

The Indian Rupee (INR) and Nepalese Rupee (NPR) share a unique fixed exchange rate of 1 INR = 1.60 NPR by official agreement. This peg eliminates volatility and simplifies cross-border payments, travel, and trade between India and Nepal.

⏳ Historical Context

The peg has remained at 1.60 NPR since the early 1990s, making INR to NPR one of the world’s most stable currency pairs.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

🔹 Is INR to NPR really fixed?

Yes, official peg by Nepal Rastra Bank: 1 INR = 1.60 NPR.

🔹 How to manually convert NPR to INR?

Divide NPR by 1.60. Example: 500 NPR ÷ 1.60 = 312.50 INR.

🔹 Can I exchange INR in Nepal?

Yes, but notes of ₹500 and ₹2000 are restricted. Lower denominations are widely accepted.

🔹 Will this rate change soon?

The peg has been stable for decades; no immediate change expected.

🔹 Is the converter accurate for large amounts?

Absolutely. The factor 1.60 works for any amount, from 1 to millions.
