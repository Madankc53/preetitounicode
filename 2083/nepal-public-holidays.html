Nepal Public Holidays 2083 BS — सार्वजनिक बिदा सूची २०८३ | Official MoHA List

This is the complete official list of Nepal public holidays 2083 BS (2026/2027 AD), published in the Nepal Gazette (Nepal Rajpatra), Volume 75, Falgun 18, 2082 BS (March 2, 2026) by the Ministry of Home Affairs (MoHA). The gazette confirms 52 total public holidays from Baisakh 1, 2083 to Chaitra end, 2083 (April 14, 2026 to April 13, 2027).

Saturday (शनिबार) and Sunday (आइतबार) are both Nepal's official weekly holidays. All 52 Saturdays and 52 Sundays are public holidays — 104 weekly off days per year. Friday is a regular working day. Nepal government office hours 2083 BS: Summer (Baisakh 1 – Kartik 15, Apr 14 – Nov 1): 9:00 AM–5:00 PM. Winter (Kartik 16 – Magh 15, Nov 2 – Jan 29): 9:00 AM–4:00 PM. Spring (Magh 16 – Chaitra end, Jan 30 – Apr 13): 9:00 AM–5:00 PM. Fridays all year: 10:00 AM–3:00 PM. Lunch break: 1:30–2:00 PM.
Dashain 2083 official holiday period: Ghatasthapana: Sunday, October 11, 2026 (Ashwin 25). Main Dashain holiday: Ashwin 31 to Kartik 6 (October 17–23, 2026) — that is 7 consecutive days from Phulpati to Dwadashi. Vijaya Dashami tika: October 21, 2026 at 11:53 AM NST (Kartik 4, 2083).
Tihar holiday 2083: Kartik 22 to Kartik 26, 2083 BS — November 8 to November 12, 2026. This is 5 days covering Laxmi Puja (Nov 8) through the day after Bhai Tika (Nov 12). Bhai Tika itself falls on Wednesday, November 11, 2026.
