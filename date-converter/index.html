How Does BS ↔ AD Conversion Work?

Bikram Sambat is a lunisolar calendar where each month can have 28–32 days depending on the year. This tool uses the exact day-offset method — counting precise days from a fixed anchor date. No formulas, no estimates, no errors.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Bikram Sambat (BS)?
Bikram Sambat is Nepal's official national calendar, a lunisolar system running approximately 56 years and 8.5 months ahead of the Gregorian calendar. For example, 2083 BS corresponds to 2026 AD.
How accurate is this converter?
This tool uses verified exact BS month-day data for every year from 2000 to 2090 BS, with the fixed anchor 2000-01-01 BS = 1943-04-14 AD. Results match Hamro Patro exactly.
Why do BS months have different days each year?
Bikram Sambat is lunisolar, so month lengths are adjusted to align with solar and lunar cycles. A BS month can have 28–32 days. The exact count must be looked up from a verified table — never estimated.
What BS years are supported?
2000 BS to 2090 BS, covering approximately 1943 AD to 2034 AD — enough for all practical needs including birth certificates, passports, and government documents.
How do I convert 2083 Jestha 13 BS to AD?
Select BS→AD, type year 2083, choose month Jestha (02), type day 13. Result: May 27, 2026 AD (Wednesday). The converter works instantly as you type.
