How Does BS to AD Conversion Work?

Bikram Sambat (BS) is a lunisolar calendar approximately 56 years and 8.5 months ahead of the Gregorian calendar. Unlike the Gregorian calendar, BS months do not have a fixed number of days — a single month can have anywhere from 28 to 32 days depending on the year.

This converter uses the exact day-offset method: every BS year and month has a precise, verified day count stored in our lookup table. Starting from the fixed base date 2000-01-01 BS = 1943-04-14 AD, the tool counts the exact number of days between the input date and the anchor, then adds that offset to the corresponding Gregorian date. No formulas. No estimates. No errors.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Bikram Sambat (BS)?
Bikram Sambat is the official national calendar of Nepal. It is a lunisolar calendar named after the legendary king Bikramaditya. BS runs approximately 56 years and 8.5 months ahead of the Gregorian (AD) calendar. For example, 2081 BS corresponds to 2024–2025 AD.
How accurate is this BS to AD converter?
This tool uses verified exact BS month-day data for every year from 2000 to 2090 BS, anchored at the fixed reference date 2000-01-01 BS = 1943-04-14 AD. Results match Hamro Patro and official Nepali government calendars. It never uses approximation formulas.
Which BS years does this tool support?
The converter supports Bikram Sambat years 2000 BS to 2090 BS, which covers approximately 1943 AD to 2034 AD in the Gregorian calendar — enough for all practical purposes including birth certificates, passports, and government documents.
Why does a BS month have different days each year?
Bikram Sambat is a lunisolar calendar, meaning month lengths are adjusted to align with both solar and lunar cycles. Each BS month can have 28, 29, 30, 31, or even 32 days. The exact length changes year to year and must be looked up from a verified calendar table.
How do I convert 2081 BS to AD?
Select "BS → AD" tab, choose year 2081, select month Baisakh (month 1), and pick day 1. Result: April 13, 2024 AD (Saturday). Every date in 2081 BS can be looked up the same way — instantly and accurately.
