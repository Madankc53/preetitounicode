What is Bikram Sambat (BS)?

Bikram Sambat is Nepal's official national calendar, named after King Vikramaditya. It runs approximately 56 years and 8 months ahead of the Gregorian calendar. BS months are lunisolar — lengths vary from 28 to 32 days each year — which is why exact lookup tables are essential. This converter uses a verified dataset for all 91 years from 2000 to 2090 BS.

What is the difference between BS and AD calendars?
AD (Gregorian) is the internationally used calendar with fixed months (28–31 days). BS (Bikram Sambat) is Nepal's official lunisolar calendar, running approximately 56 years 8 months ahead of AD, with variable month lengths of 28–32 days.
