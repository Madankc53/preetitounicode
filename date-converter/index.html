What Is Bikram Sambat (BS)?

Bikram Sambat (BS) — also written Vikram Samvat — is Nepal's official national calendar, named after the legendary King Vikramaditya. It runs approximately 56 years and 8 months ahead of the Gregorian (AD) calendar. So 2081 BS corresponds roughly to 2024–2025 AD.

Unlike the Gregorian calendar where every month has a predictable number of days, BS months are lunisolar — meaning month lengths vary from year to year, ranging between 28 and 32 days. For example, Baisakh 2080 BS had 31 days, while Baisakh 2081 BS had 31 days too — but Jestha 2080 had 32 days. This is why accurate conversion requires exact pre-computed lookup tables, not estimation formulas.

This converter uses a day-offset method anchored to the verified base reference: 2000-01-01 BS = 1943-04-14 AD. Every conversion counts the exact number of days between the target date and this anchor — matching the accuracy of the Hamro Patro calendar.
